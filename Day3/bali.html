Introduction

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches and coral reefs. It offers a mix of vibrant culture, stunning nature, and relaxing beaches that attract travelers from around the world.

Top 5 Things to Do (Suggested Order)

  1. Visit Ubud

    Explore the cultural heart of Bali — art galleries, the Monkey Forest, and terraced rice fields nearby.

  2. Watch the Sunset at Tanah Lot

    See the temple silhouette against a dramatic coastal sunset — an iconic Bali experience.

  3. Relax on Seminyak or Nusa Dua Beaches

    Choose Seminyak for lively cafes and beach clubs; Nusa Dua for calm waters and resort life.

  4. Hike Mount Batur (Sunrise Trek)

    Start very early to catch sunrise from the volcano summit — a challenging but rewarding trek.

  5. Discover Water Temples & Rice Terraces

    Visit Tirta Empul (a sacred spring temple) and the famous Tegallalang rice terraces for scenic walks and photos.

Quick Tips

Respect local customs: dress modestly when visiting temples and remove shoes where required.

Transport: Renting a scooter is common, but only if you're comfortable riding abroad — otherwise hire a driver for day trips.

Best time to visit: April to October (dry season) for outdoor activities and beach time.
